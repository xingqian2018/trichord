## Extended Watchlist — Smaller / Non-S&P 500 Companies

## Notes

- **Review cadence**: revisit this list quarterly or when a company has a material event (IPO graduation to S&P 500, M&A, fundamental inflection).
- **Graduation**: if a company joins the S&P 500, remove it from this list (it's covered by default scope).
- **Removal criteria**: remove a company if the original thesis is broken (not just because the stock is down).
